Jimmy Stokes is a licensed, versatile hairdresser who carries nearly 40 years of experience behind the chair.  He co-owned and operated Hair Stop Family Haircutters in Gentilly for 29 years.  Jimmy specializes in all aspects of modern hairdressing including precision hair cuts, color, highlights, clipper cutting, relaxers, permanent waves, and waxing for all ethnicities. He has been at Le Parc for four and a half years.
